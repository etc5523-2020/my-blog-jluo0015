Introduction

Although Japan has dense and aging population, it still reported a low cases of COVID-19 infection in the first few months of the COVID-19 outbreak, compared with other countries (akiko). However, according to the latest COVID-19 report from Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare of Japan, Japan has experience the second outbreak of COVID-19 from July, 2020. And it is more serious than the first one. Therefore, this post will focus on the COVID-19 cases of Japan.
